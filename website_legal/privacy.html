Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 30, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to STIRA. We respect your privacy and are deeply committed to protecting your personal data. STIRA is built on a Local-First Architecture, meaning the vast majority of your behavioral data never leaves your device.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the STIRA mobile application ("App").

2. Information We Collect

Because STIRA is designed for maximum privacy, we severely limit what data leaves your device.

  • Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your email address, password (securely hashed), or authentication tokens from third-party providers (Google or Apple).
  • App Usage & Analytics: We collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics and crash reports to improve the App's stability and performance.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic device information (e.g., OS version, device model) solely for troubleshooting and analytics.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect (Local Storage)

The core of STIRA's behavioral intelligence runs directly on your device. The following data is stored locally (using secure on-device databases like Hive) and is never transmitted to our servers or third parties:

  • Daily Check-In responses (Urge intensity, triggers, energy levels)
  • Dopamine Journal entries
  • "The Vault" letters and personal nudges
  • Precise behavioral forecasting and vulnerability windows computed by the Stira Intelligence Engine.

4. Third-Party Services

To facilitate account management and ensure app stability, we use the following trusted third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication: For secure user sign-up and login.
  • Firebase Crashlytics & Analytics: To monitor app health, detect crashes, and understand aggregate feature usage.
  • Supabase: For secure authentication or specific cloud synchronization features (if enabled by you).

5. Security

We implement robust security measures:

  • Local data is stored securely on your device.
  • You can secure access to STIRA using your device's native biometrics (FaceID/Fingerprint).
  • Network communications for authentication are encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.

6. Your Rights & Data Deletion

  • Data Export: You have the right to request an export of any cloud-stored account data.
  • Data Deletion: You can delete your local data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing the app data. You can delete your cloud account and associated authentication data directly through the App settings or via our Account Deletion page.

7. Children's Privacy

STIRA is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
