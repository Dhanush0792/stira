Data Deletion Policy

This page outlines how you can delete specific data without deleting your entire STIRA account.

Local Data (Hive Database)

STIRA is a local-first application. Your most sensitive data (Dopamine Journal entries, Daily Check-ins) is stored exclusively on your device's local Hive database.

To delete your local data:

  1. Open your device settings.
  2. Navigate to Apps > STIRA.
  3. Select Clear Data / Clear Storage.

Note: This will reset the app to its factory state, erasing all your local behavioral history.

Cloud Analytics & Crash Data

We use Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics. This data is collected anonymously. We do not tie specific crash reports to your identifiable email address.

Requesting Deletion of Synced Data

If you have utilized cloud backup/syncing features, you can request the deletion of that specific synced data while maintaining your login credentials.

To request specific data deletion:

  • Email: missionhousehq@gmail.com
  • Subject: Data Deletion Request
  • Detail what specific data you want purged from our systems.

Verification Requirements

For your security, we require that data deletion requests be sent from the email address registered to your STIRA account. We may ask for additional verification before processing the deletion.
