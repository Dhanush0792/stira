# Support & FAQ

Welcome to the STIRA Support Center. We are here to help you get the most out of your behavioral stability journey.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**1. How does the Stira Intelligence Engine work?**
The Stira Intelligence Engine runs completely locally on your device. Every 15 minutes, it analyzes your daily check-ins and logged triggers to predict your highly vulnerable hours and trigger proactive notifications to help you stay stable.

**2. Are my journal entries and check-ins private?**
Yes. STIRA uses a **local-first architecture**. Your daily check-ins, Dopamine Journal entries, and The Vault letters are stored locally on your device using Hive NoSQL. They are not uploaded to our servers.

**3. What happens if I get a new phone?**
If you enabled cloud synchronization during setup (via Supabase/Firebase), your core statistics and settings can be restored. However, any purely local data not synced will be lost unless you perform a manual backup.



## Troubleshooting
- **Notifications not arriving?** Please check your device settings and ensure you haven't disabled notifications for STIRA. Also, the app limits notifications to prevent fatigue, enforcing quiet hours and a minimum 3-hour gap between alerts.
- **App crashing?** Ensure you are on the latest version. Crash reports are anonymously sent via Firebase Crashlytics to help us fix bugs rapidly.
