# Tracking & Analytics Disclosure

STIRA is committed to transparency. While our core behavioral tracking is 100% local to your device, we use certain third-party tracking and telemetry tools to ensure the app functions correctly and securely.

## 1. What We Collect & Why

### A. Firebase Analytics
We collect high-level, anonymized usage statistics to understand how users interact with STIRA. 
* **What is collected:** App open rates, screen views, and button interactions (e.g., how often the 90-Second Reset is used).
* **Why:** To determine which features are most beneficial so we can improve the app.
* **Anonymity:** We do not connect this analytics data to your sensitive local journal entries or specific behavioral check-in details.

### B. Firebase Crashlytics
When the app crashes or encounters a fatal error, an automatic report is generated.
* **What is collected:** Device model, OS version, app version, and the specific line of code that caused the crash.
* **Why:** To allow our development team to identify and fix bugs rapidly.

### C. Usage_Stats (App Usage)
STIRA may request permissions to monitor your general device usage statistics.
* **What is collected:** Screen time and app launch frequency.
* **Why:** To help the local Stira Intelligence Engine calculate behavioral correlations (e.g., if excessive screen time correlates with higher urge intensity). **This specific analysis happens purely locally.**

## 2. Third-Party Providers
We utilize the following providers for telemetry and analytics:
* **Google (Firebase):** [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## 3. User Controls & Data Protection
You can opt-out of analytics and crash reporting at any time through the **Profile & Settings** menu within the STIRA app. Disabling these features will not affect the core functionality of the behavioral engine.
