# Account Deletion Instructions

We value your right to control your personal data. If you no longer wish to use STIRA, you can easily delete your account.

## How to Delete Your Account

### Option 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)
1. Open the STIRA app.
2. Navigate to **Profile & Settings**.
3. Scroll to the bottom and tap **Delete Account**.
4. Confirm your choice. This action is irreversible.

### Option 2: Email Request
If you no longer have the app installed, you can request account deletion by emailing us:
* **Email:** [missionhousehq@gmail.com](mailto:missionhousehq@gmail.com)
* **Subject:** Account Deletion Request
* Please send the email from the address associated with your STIRA account for verification.

## What Happens When You Delete Your Account?
* **Firebase Authentication:** Your login credentials, email, and associated social linkings (Google/Apple) will be permanently deleted from our authentication database.
* **Cloud Data:** If you enabled cloud syncing, all associated metrics in Firebase/Supabase will be securely erased.
* **Local Data:** **Important:** Because STIRA relies heavily on local storage, any journals, check-ins, and Vault letters stored locally on your physical device must be wiped by you. **Uninstalling the STIRA app from your device will permanently delete all local data.**

## Processing Timelines
In-app deletions are processed immediately. Email requests are verified and processed within 7-14 business days.

## Recovery Limitations
Once an account is deleted, **it cannot be recovered**. All historical stability scores, streaks, and insights will be lost forever.
